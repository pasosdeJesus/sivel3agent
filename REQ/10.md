## Metrics (Noche y Niebla alignment)

| Metric | Weight | What it measures |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| **Classification accuracy** | 40% | `agresion_particular` matches CINEP code (B40, A15, D903, etc.) |
| **Geographic precision** | 20% | `departamento` + `municipio` exact match |
| **Actor identification** | 15% | `grupos` and `presuntos_responsables_individuales` present and correct |
| **JSON validity** | 10% | Parseable JSON matching `relato.schema.json` |
| **Path efficiency** | 10% | Output length, retries, hallucination markers |
| **Speed** | 5% | Seconds per article |
